About Me

Hello! I am a PhD scholar specializing in optical and optomechanical instrumentation designed for the exoplanet field, specifically focused on the discovery and characterization of exoplanets. My primary research interests lie in investigating the formation and evolution of extreme, anomalous planetary architectures.

I hold a foundational scientific conviction that a comprehensive, unified theory will one day resolve our lingering questions regarding planetary origins and evolutionary paths. My work aims to build and refine the high-precision tools necessary to unlock those answers.
